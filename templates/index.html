ABOUT

Civicsift is a platform dedicated to providing a streamlined and comprehensive search and discovery experience for government schemes. It offers a one-stop solution for citizens to access information about various government schemes tailored to their eligibility.

This platform empowers citizens by assisting them in identifying the most relevant government schemes that cater to their specific needs. Additionally, Civicsift offers guidance on the application process for different government schemes, eliminating the necessity to navigate through multiple government websites.
